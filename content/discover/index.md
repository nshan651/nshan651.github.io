Here are some great sites that I recommend:

## Blogs {#blogs}

* [Daemonology](http://www.daemonology.net/) -- Lots of writings on FreeBSD
* [Dan Luu](https://danluu.com/) -- Newsletter focusing on economics and tech 
* [Gwern](https://gwern.net) -- An absolutely **incredible** blog, so much here
* [James Sinclair](https://jrsinclair.com/) -- Austrailian web dev, lots of writings on functional programming
* [Joey Hess](http://joeyh.name/) -- Debian core maintainer
* [Jsomers](https://jsomers.net/) -- Writer and programmer based in NY
* [Lukesmith.xyz](https://lukesmith.xyz/) -- Tech, linguistics, and more
* [Mike McQuaid](https://mikemcquaid.com/) -- Principle engineer working on MacOS Homebrew
* [Neil Gaiman's Blog](https://neilgaiman.com/) -- Need I say more
* [Naima El Bassunie](https://www.niamaelbassunie.com/) -- Great perspectives on the startup scene in Africa
* [The Autodidacts](https://www.autodidacts.io/) -- Blog of three self-educating brothers
  
## Tech {#tech}

* [Arch Linux](https://archlinux.org/) -- The Arch Linux homepage and wiki
* [Gizmodo](https://gizmodo.com/) -- Tech, science, and culture
* [Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com/) -- Tech news focusing on computer science and entrepreneurship
* [Haskell in Production](https://serokell.io/blog/haskell-in-production) -- Collection of interviews of various companies and devs who use Haskell in a production environment
* [New Atlas](https://newatlas.com/) -- New science and technology news
* [No Tech Magazine](https://www.notechmagazine.com/)-- Hosts low-tech magazine posts, plus some additional content
* [Lobste.rs](https://lobste.rs) -- Another link aggregator like HN; generally more tech focused
* [Low-Tech Magazine](https://www.lowtechmagazine.com/) -- Tech magazine focusing on sustainability. Website is totally powered by solar energy
* [The Register](https://www.theregister.com/) -- IT news
* [The cheapskate's guide to computers and the internet](https://cheapskatesguide.org/)
* [Slashdot](https://www.google.com/search?q=slashdot&oq=slashdot&aqs=chrome..69i57j5.1124j0j1&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8) -- News for nerds, stuff that matters
* [2600](https://www.2600.com/)- The hacker quarterly
